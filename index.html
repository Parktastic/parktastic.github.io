Find a doctor that's right for you


Choosing the right doctor is one of the most important health decisions you'll ever make. It is a complex process that is not the same as say, choosing a taxi service.

ConnectHealth helps you find a doctor the right way: based on the specialists your doctor works with and who your friends and family consult. Engage with those trusted by your network. Find the doctor that works for you.

Follow up consults with your doctor from anywhere


As a 21st century consumer, you’re always looking for convenient solutions to accommodate a busy schedule. Why should healthcare solutions be any different?

ConnectHealth lets you fully engage with your care team securely and conveniently, over your mobile device. You can now schedule your follow up visit and get care from your doctor from the convenience of your home or on the go.

Be an active stakeholder in your loved ones' care experience


Just because you’re far away doesn’t mean you cannot be there for your loved ones when they need you.

ConnectHealth lets you be an active part of your loved ones’ doctor visits, remotely. With a click of a button you can join the consultation over a secure video call from wherever you are.
